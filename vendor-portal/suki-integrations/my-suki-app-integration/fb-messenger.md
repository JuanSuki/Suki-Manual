# FB Messenger

## Add FB Messenger URL

**(1)** At the Menu bar, Navigate to Admin > Integrations > My Suki App

**(2)** Navigate to FB Messenger Tab

**(3)** Enter the FB Messenger URL of your Store FB Page

**(4)** Click Update to save the changes
